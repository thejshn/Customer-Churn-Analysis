StreamWorks Media – Customer Churn Analysis

Overview

Analysed customer churn behaviour for StreamWorks Media using data cleaning, feature engineering, statistical analysis, and machine learning.

The project explored how customer engagement, loyalty, promotions, and subscription behaviour influence churn risk and customer retention.

⸻

Key Objectives

* Identify drivers of customer churn
* Analyse customer engagement patterns
* Evaluate the impact of promotions on retention
* Build a churn prediction model
* Generate business recommendations

⸻

Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Scikit-learn
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

⸻

Key Insights

* Promotions showed a significant relationship with customer retention
* Low engagement and short subscription tenure increased churn risk
* Loyal customers demonstrated stronger retention patterns
* Watch time alone was not a strong predictor of churn

⸻

Model Highlights

* Built a Logistic Regression churn prediction model
* Identified key churn drivers:
    * Watch-to-Fee Ratio
    * Loyalty Status
    * Engagement Levels

⸻

Business Recommendations

* Re-engage low activity users through personalised campaigns
* Expand loyalty and retention programs
* Improve mobile app engagement and user experience
